About us

Financial Press Limited

A REFRESHED NAME WITH FAMILIAR FACES

With the extensive industry knowledge and experience in the financial printing market, JAN Financial Press Limited is confident to provide world-class services for our clients in both primary market and secondary market.

Our Services

Financial Printing Services:

In the primary market, IPO prospectuses are the most important documents to connect issuers and investors. As a preferred financial printer, we recognize that accuracy and efficiency in typesetting and translation are crucial and our teams uphold these high standards at all times.

In the secondary market, financial statements & ESG information, circulars and announcement & notices are the regular disclosures of listed companies. Our teams offer a wide array of service solutions ranging from design, typesetting, translation, printing and uploading the documents to the websites of the regulators and clients on a 24/7, round-the-clock basis.

Other Services:

Besides the traditional financial printing services, we also offer web hosting services to listed companies and provide conference room facilities to cater for clients’ needs for organizing meetings, training sessions and seminars.
